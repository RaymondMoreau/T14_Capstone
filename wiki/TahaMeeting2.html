What Kind of Games Client Wants:

  • Games that can be completed quickly
  • Games that require thinking and problem-solving
  • Games with animation through different stages
  • Progression-based games where players build something as they get correct answers
  • Boss game where players damage the boss over time with more difficult questions doing more damage

General Notes:

  • Game template: Easily adaptable with switchable images and questions
  • Randomized answers close to the correct answer
  • Possibility of false answers based on common mistakes (more difficult to implement)
  • Escape room ideas as games with changing storylines and values/questions
  • Practice games for small additions/subtractions/multiplications/divisions - starting basic and increasing in difficulty
  • Potential features: Teacher checks on student games played in a week, games with video interaction
